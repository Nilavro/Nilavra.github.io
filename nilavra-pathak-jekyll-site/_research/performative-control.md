title: "Performative Control for Marketing Systems"
summary: "Control-oriented modeling of decision systems where actions change the future environment."
---

This research direction focuses on decision systems where the policy does not merely observe the environment, but actively changes it.

In marketing systems, budget, bidding, pacing, and target-setting decisions can affect future delivery, coverage, conversion dynamics, and measured returns. This motivates a performative-control view of marketing optimization.

Key themes include:

- Feedback control for marketing decisions.
- Non-stationary response dynamics.
- Human-in-the-loop target setting.
- Stability constraints.
- Sequential policy evaluation.
- Simulation environments for stress testing.
