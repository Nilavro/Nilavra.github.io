This research direction focuses on estimating how marketing spend translates into business outcomes and using those estimates to support budget allocation, bidding, and campaign automation.

The work combines machine learning, system identification, and control ideas to model spend-response dynamics under noisy, non-stationary, and operationally constrained environments.

Key themes include:

- Spend-response modeling.
- ROAS and marginal-return estimation.
- Budget optimization.
- Feedback control for business targets.
- Automation of manual campaign operations.
- Production-scale decision support.
