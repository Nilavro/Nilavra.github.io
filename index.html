About

Decision systems for real-world allocation problems

I am Nilavra Pathak, a Senior Machine Learning Scientist at Expedia Group, where I work on large-scale machine learning and decision systems for marketing science.

My work sits at the intersection of machine learning, economics, system identification, and control. I am interested in replacing heuristic, manually tuned business decision-making with systematic, measurable, and feedback-driven decision systems.

In practice, this includes marketing budget allocation, bidding, campaign automation, response-curve forecasting, counterfactual measurement, regret-aware policy evaluation, and semi-synthetic simulation.

Before joining Expedia Group, my doctoral research focused on energy and building analytics, using probabilistic machine learning and system identification to model building systems.

Research portfolio

Selected research directions

01

Response Curve Forecasting and Budget Optimization

Forecasting marketing spend-response curves and using them for scalable budget allocation, bidding, and campaign automation systems.

02

Certified Retrospective Regret

Auditing historical allocation decisions using model-based hindsight oracles that respect budget, stability, and operational constraints.

03

Nonparametric Marginality Zone Detection

Distributional methods for identifying spend regions where marginal gains become locally weak, ambiguous, or economically indifferent.

04

Performative Control for Marketing Systems

Control-oriented modeling of decision systems where budget, bidding, pacing, and targeting decisions change the future environment.

05

Energy Economist and DataCenterGym Extensions

Semi-synthetic decision environments for evaluating energy, data center, smart-city, and infrastructure policy questions.
